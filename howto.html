How to Use

  • Select a Tool: Choose the pen or eraser from the toolbar.
  • Start Drawing: Touch the canvas and hold for 1.5 seconds until the tooltip changes color to green, then move to draw.
  • Undo/Redo: Use the undo and redo buttons to step backward or forward through your actions.
  • Copy Drawing: Click the copy button to save your drawing to the clipboard. Paste it into any application that supports images.
  • Clear Canvas: Use the trash icon to clear the entire canvas.
